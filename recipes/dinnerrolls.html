Dinner Rolls

Description

Soft, chewy and more fluffy than a college essay reaching a word count. Perfect with friends (if any)

Ingredients

  • 5 cups all purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of instant dry yeast
  • 3/4 cups butter (room temperature)
  • 2 cups water (Almost uncomfortable warm)

Steps:

  1. In large bowl, add flour, sugar and instant dry yeast.
  2. In bowl with dry ingredients, add 3/4 cups of butter and squish together with hand until lumps are smaller than a marble
  3. Add water to large bowl and mix together until dough becomes one gelotinous mess.
  4. Grease dough with shortening, cover with tea cloth and let sit for 30 minutes.
  5. After 30 minutes, come back and punch dough, kneading all the air out. Cover and let raise for another 30 minutes.
  6. Take out dough, punch down slightly, cut into 16 pieces. Covering each piece with grease and put in baking pan.
  7. Cover buns in pan with tea cloth. Let sit for 30 minutes. Meanwhile pre-heat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  8. After 30 minutes, put buns in oven for 22 minute or until tops are golden brown.
